Services

Our Services

Machine Repairs and Maintenance

We conduct repairs to all machine parts e.g Engine.We also carry out Machine inspectioniorder to advise a client o what repairs and changes should be made in the machine.

Diagnostics & Repairs

We conduct accurate troubleshooting for problem identification with the aid of our computer diagnostic equipment. Our repairs are therefore guided by facts and not mechanic guess work or speculation. This is to ensure minimal down time of our client’s machines. We also offer routine maintenance and servicing to our clients all the while ensuring economic running of our client’s machinery.

Hydraulic Systems

We are experts in troubleshooting, repair and servicing of hydraulic cylinders applicable in various machines and for multiple uses e.g for tipping jacks for tippers, boom cylinders for excavators, wheel loaders and backhoe loaders, cylinders for motor graders e.t.c

Construction Machinery Spare Parts

We offer a wide range of spare parts for earth moving equipment including original and aftermarket engine parts for all brands at best affordable market price. We endeavor to apply a special warranty to our clients on our parts hence safeguarding our client’s purchases.

Hydraulic Hose & Car wash Pipe and Fittings

Hydraulics have multiple applications across a wide range of heavy equipment. In this regard we sell new Hydraulic pipes, we offer skilled solutions in repairing worn out hydraulic Hose pipes & their fittings and replacement for those beyond repair.

Air compressors and Hydraulic cylinders

CAE is also well versed in servicing and repair of air compressors and their respective components including drilling rigs applicable for multiple uses. We also stock spare parts for the different brands of these air compressors including Atlas Copco, ELGI, TEWATT, e.t.c
