Our Story

Countrywide Autotrack Enterprises(CAE) is a company that deals in supply of Road Construction Machinery and Spare parts.It was established in 1999 by Catapillar dealer trained Engineers.CAE was formed to provide Quality Services and products to varied clients in Building Construction Industry, Road Construction, Contractors, Industrial and Agricultural establishments, Non-Govermental Organizations both National and County Governments.

    Hydraulic Hose & Car wash Pipe and Fittings

    In our endeavors to increase our professionalism within the industry we introduced Hydraulic Hoses and Fittings as part of our services offered to our clients.

    This was done over 15 years ago and was aimed to offer our clients a combination of services under one roof. Hydraulics have multiple applications across a wide range of heavy equipment. In this regard, we offer skilled solutions in repairing worn out Hydraulic Hose pipes and their fittings, and replacement for those beyond repair.

    • We are stockists of all sizes of Hydraulic Hoses and Car wash Pipes
    • Repairing worn out and damaged pipes
    • Replacing and repairing fittings.

    Air compressors and Hydraulic cylinders

    CAE is also well versed in servicing and repair of air compressors and their respective components including drilling rigs applicable for multiple uses. We also stock spare parts for the different brands of these air compressors including Atlas Copco, ELGI, TEWATT, e.t.c

    Diagnostics,Repairing and Servicing of all heavy equipment.

    With our vast experience of more than 20 years in handling heavy equipment, we are well placed to offer to our client’s technical advice, servicing and repairs of all heavy equipment.

    Led by our experienced Engineers in our technical team we conduct accurate troubleshooting for problem identification with the aid of our computer diagnostic equipment in our possession. Our repairs are therefore guided by facts and not mechanic guess work or speculation. This is to ensure minimal down time of our client’s machines. We also offer routine maintenance and servicing to our clients all the while ensuring economic running of our client’s machinery.

    • We run computer aided diagnostics
