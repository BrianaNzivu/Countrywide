Our Story

Countrywide Autotrack Enterprises(CAE) is a company that deals in supply of Road Construction Machinery and Spare parts.It was established in 1999 by Catapillar dealer trained Engineers.CAE was formed to provide Quality Services and products to varied clients in Building Construction Industry, Road Construction, Contractors, Industrial and Agricultural establishments, Non-Govermental Organizations both National and County Governments.
